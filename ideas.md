<idea>
  **Design Movement**: Cyberpunk Neo-Brutalism
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Raw Data Visualization**: Expose the underlying logic and data streams directly in the UI.
  2. **High Contrast & Neon**: Use deep blacks with piercing neon accents (green/orange) to create a sense of urgency and precision.
  3. **Modular Grid Systems**: Everything aligns to a strict, visible grid, reminiscent of terminal interfaces but modernized.
  4. **Glitch & Distortion**: Subtle glitch effects on interactions to emphasize the digital nature of the tool.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Deep Void Black (#050505)**: Represents the unknown digital space.
  - **Neon Cyber Green (#00FF41)**: Represents safety, verification, and "go" signals. Used for safe verdicts.
  - **Warning Orange (#FF5F00)**: Represents caution, alerts, and high-risk areas. Used for critical verdicts.
  - **Metallic Silver (#C0C0C0)**: For structural elements and typography, giving a cold, industrial feel.
  - *Psychology*: High contrast creates focus and alertness. Green/Orange dichotomy simplifies complex decisions into binary safety signals.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Asymmetric Dashboard**: Avoid centered containers. Use a sidebar-heavy layout with a "command center" feel.
  - **Split-Screen Analysis**: Input on left, real-time data stream on right.
  - **Floating HUD Elements**: Critical stats (risk score) float above the content layer.

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Scanlines**: Subtle CRT scanline overlay on the background.
  - **Monospace Typography**: Use a high-quality monospace font for all data and analysis text.
  - **Data Borders**: Borders that look like circuit traces or data pipes.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Instant Feedback**: Every keystroke triggers a micro-analysis visualization.
  - **Haptic Visuals**: Buttons "press" deep into the screen; toggles snap with mechanical precision.

  **Animation**:
  - **Typewriter Effect**: Analysis results appear character-by-character.
  - **Glitch Transitions**: Page changes involve a brief digital distortion.
  - **Pulse**: Risk indicators pulse like a heartbeat.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headlines**: 'Space Grotesk' or 'Orbitron' (Bold, Uppercase, Metallic effect).
  - **Body/Data**: 'JetBrains Mono' or 'Fira Code' (Readable, Technical).
</idea>

<idea>
  **Design Movement**: Swiss International Style (Digital Remaster)
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Absolute Clarity**: Content is king. No decorative elements that don't serve a function.
  2. **Strict Grid**: A visible or invisible grid that governs every pixel.
  3. **Bold Typography**: Large, impactful type used as a graphical element.
  4. **High Contrast B&W + Accents**: Mainly black and white, with strategic use of green/orange.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Stark White (#FFFFFF)**: Background.
  - **Ink Black (#000000)**: Text and primary structure.
  - **Signal Green (#00D26A)**: Safety.
  - **Alert Orange (#FF4D00)**: Risk.
  - *Psychology*: Clinical, professional, trustworthy. Removes emotion to focus on facts.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Modular Grid**: Blocks of content arranged in a strict grid.
  - **Typographic Hierarchy**: Size and weight define importance, not color or decoration.

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Thick Dividers**: Bold black lines separating sections.
  - **Huge Numbers**: Risk scores are massive, taking up significant space.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Snap**: Interactions are instant and sharp.
  - **Hover Invert**: Buttons invert colors on hover.

  **Animation**:
  - **Slide**: Panels slide in from sides.
  - **Reveal**: Content is revealed from behind masks.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headlines**: 'Helvetica Now' or 'Arial' (Bold, Tight tracking).
  - **Body**: 'Helvetica Now' or 'Arial' (Regular).
</idea>
